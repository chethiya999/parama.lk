Our Recycling Process

A streamlined approach to turn your plastic waste into new materials

1

Collection & Transport

We collect plastic waste from your location using our specialized vehicles and transport it to our processing facility.

2

Sorting & Cleaning

Advanced sorting technology separates different plastic types, followed by thorough cleaning to remove contaminants.

3

Shredding & Melting

Clean plastics are shredded into flakes and melted down using energy-efficient processes.

4

Pelletizing

Melted plastic is formed into pellets that serve as raw material for manufacturing new products.

5

Quality Testing

All recycled materials undergo rigorous quality control testing to ensure they meet industry standards.

6

Distribution

Recycled plastic pellets are distributed to manufacturers to create new products, completing the circular economy.
