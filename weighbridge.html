Our Weighing Process

Streamlined and accurate weighing procedures for all vehicle types

1

Vehicle Registration

Register your vehicle details and obtain a unique ticket number for tracking and documentation purposes.

2

Entry Weighing

Drive onto the weighbridge platform for initial weighing (gross weight) with load or empty weight measurement.

3

Load Verification

Verify load details, destination, and any special handling requirements for accurate documentation.

4

Weight Recording

Digital weight recording with automatic data capture and integration with management systems.

5

Exit Weighing

Final weighing upon exit to determine net weight and calculate actual load transported.

6

Certificate Issue

Issue certified weight certificates and digital receipts for legal compliance and record keeping.
