Frequently Asked Questions

What services does PARAMA Industries offer?

We offer comprehensive industrial solutions including weighbridge services, plastic recycling, PVC manufacturing, gardening solutions, logistics services, and industrial consulting through our network of subsidiary companies.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, our emergency hotline is available 24/7. Our team prioritizes quick response times to ensure your business operations continue smoothly.

Do you provide customized solutions?

Yes, we specialize in tailored solutions that meet specific client requirements. Our team works closely with you to understand your needs and develop customized approaches that optimize efficiency and cost-effectiveness.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the Sri Lankan market with our main operations based in Colombo. However, we also provide services to selected international markets depending on the project scope and requirements.

How can I get a quote for services?

You can request a quote by filling out our contact form above, calling our sales team, or emailing us directly. We'll need some basic project details to provide an accurate estimate and timeline.

Do you offer maintenance and support services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance and support services for all our solutions. This includes regular maintenance schedules, emergency repairs, technical support, and system upgrades to ensure optimal performance.
