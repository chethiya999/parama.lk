About PARAMA Industries

Leading the way in sustainable industrial solutions with innovative technology and environmental responsibility

About Parama Group Sri Lanka

Parama Group has grown over two decades into one of Sri Lanka’s trusted names in PVC manufacturing, plastic recycling, and industrial services.

Headquartered in Panapitiya, Waskaduwa, we combine modern machinery, skilled teams, and sustainable practices to help clients across Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

Building up a greenish environment by generating new job opportunities through the development of industries in order to save foreign currency.

Our Vision

Reducing the usage of plastic by recycling the disposable plastic item without affecting to the environment.

Our Goals

Reducing the high rates of pollution that cause environmental problems. Putting less pressure on virgin materials to produce new plastic products. Making new job opportunities for the younger with earning profit under the greenish environment in Sri Lanka.
