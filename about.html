About PARAMA Industries

Leading the way in sustainable industrial solutions with innovative technology and environmental responsibility

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize industrial manufacturing through sustainable practices, PARAMA Industries has grown from a small startup to a leading force in green technology solutions.

We specialize in weighbridge services, plastic recycling, PVC manufacturing, and innovative industrial solutions that prioritize both quality and environmental responsibility.

Our commitment to excellence and sustainability has earned us the trust of over 250 business partners and helped us process more than 150,000 vehicles through our weighbridge services.

Our Industrial Facility

Our Mission

To provide innovative, sustainable industrial solutions that drive economic growth while protecting our environment. We are committed to delivering high-quality products and services that exceed customer expectations and contribute to a greener future.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable industrial solutions in the region, recognized for our innovation, quality, and commitment to environmental stewardship. We envision a future where industrial growth and environmental protection go hand in hand.

Managing Director

Message from Our Managing Director

"At PARAMA Industries, we believe that sustainable growth and environmental responsibility are not just business imperatives, but moral obligations to future generations."

Since founding PARAMA Industries over a decade ago, our journey has been guided by a simple yet powerful vision: to create industrial solutions that benefit both business and the environment. What started as a small venture has grown into a comprehensive network of companies serving diverse industrial needs across the region.

Our success stems from our unwavering commitment to three core principles: innovation in sustainable technology, excellence in service delivery, and integrity in all our business relationships. These values have enabled us to build lasting partnerships with over 250 businesses while maintaining our position as industry leaders in weighbridge services, plastic recycling, and PVC manufacturing.

As we look toward the future, we remain dedicated to expanding our impact through continuous innovation and strategic growth. Our subsidiary companies represent our commitment to providing comprehensive solutions under one trusted umbrella, ensuring our clients receive the highest quality service across all their industrial needs.

I am proud of what we have accomplished together, and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. Thank you for being part of our journey toward a more sustainable and prosperous future.

Rajesh Parama

Managing Director & Founder
